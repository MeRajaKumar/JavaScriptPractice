Indian Railways inducts 100th Electric Locomotive of 12000 HP WAG 12B

12000 HP WAG 12 B has been produced under the Make in India programme and manufactured by "Madhepura Electric Locomotive Pvt. Ltd."

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________


NEW DELHI, India (Metro Rail News): Indian Railways inducted 100th Electric Locomotive of

12000 HP WAG 12 B in its fleet. The loco is named WAG 12 B with the number 60100. The locomotive

is manufactured by Madhepura Electric Locomotive Pvt. Ltd. (MELPL). The Electric Locomotive

Factory, Madhepura is a joint venture of Alstom SA of France with Indian Railways for the production

of 800 high-power locomotives over a period of 11 years designed to run on Indian tracks at 120 km per

hour. The manufacturing has started on 11 October 2017.


Hightlights:

1. The locomotive has been produced under the Make in India programme

2. The locomotive is manufactured by Madhepura Electric Locomotive Pvt. Ltd. (MELPL)
